About

Hi, I'm Kasper, a gameplay programmer currently studying in Finland.

I started my game development journey in 2020, and since then, I've found that I work best in a systematic and iterative way. Right now, I'm most comfortable with action-oriented games, but I enjoy working on all kinds of projects and the unique challenges that come with them.

On the technical side, I am most familiar working with Unity and C#. I have some experience using Unreal & Godot as well, and of course, I'm always up for learning new tools.

In my free time, I like participating in game jams. As of now, I've submitted to over 15! I also like to experiment with fields outside of programming, like with art and audio. In addition to just being fun, I think it helps me work better with everyone.

I'm currently looking for an intership to finish my education, so if you're interested, feel free to reach out! Looking forward to hearing from you!
